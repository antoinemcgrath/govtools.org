Privacy Statement

While using GovTools.org you are releasing collectable information that may be gathered. GovTools.org uses Google Analytics to track visitation and usage. This 3rd party tracks the domain name of each visitor and each Visitor’s IP address, web pages requested and associated time stamps. Google Analytics collects this information, removes Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and provides the analysis to GovTools.org. Google Analytics is owned by Google and their privacy policy can be read here.

In further detail GovTools.org through Google Analytics uses web cookies to track user visits and activities on the site. This is common practice for data mining that may be used to further improve this website. Cookies are small files used by websites and frequently transferred to visiting computers. You may remove and/or block cookies from websites (visit you settings/preferences in your browser for more information). While using a modern browser you may activate privacy/incognito mode and still use our website, in this mode your computer will only temporarily store cookies and automatically remove them once the session is ended.
