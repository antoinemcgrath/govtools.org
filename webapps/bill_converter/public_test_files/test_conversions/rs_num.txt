 8     (b) DEFINITIONS.—In this section:
 9         (1) APPROPRIATE CONGRESSIONAL COMMIT-
 10     TEES.—The term ‘‘appropriate congressional com-
 11     mittees’’ means the Committee on Foreign Relations
 12     of the Senate and the Committee on Foreign Affairs
 13     of the House of Representatives.
 14         (2)  STATE  SPONSOR  OF  TERRORISM  DE-
 15     FINED.—The term ‘‘state sponsor of terrorism’’
 16     means a country the government of which the Sec-
 17     retary of State has determined, for purposes of sec-
 18     tion 6(j) of the Export Administration Act of 1979
 19     (50 U.S.C. 4605(j)) (as in effect pursuant to the
 20     International Emergency Economic Powers Act),
 21     section 620A of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961
 22     (22 U.S.C. 2371), section 40 of the Arms Export
 23     Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2780), or any other provi-
 24     sion of law, is a government that has repeatedly pro-
 25     vided support for acts of international terrorism.
